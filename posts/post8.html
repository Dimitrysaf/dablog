video footage, were published in National Geographic Magazine,
overlaid with subtitles suggesting: "This is what climate change

quickly clocking up an estimated 2.5 billion views, and fuelling
a global discussion about the threat of melting ice caps and global warming.


Images of distressed polar bears clinging to ice floes or in
far-flung Arctic landscapes have become instantly recognisable as
emblems for the climate crisis. However, over the last decade,
scientists, campaigners and news desks have begun to drift away from
these visuals, questioning if they really paint a realistic image of climate change.
